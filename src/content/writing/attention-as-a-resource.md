# Attention as a Resource

We live in an attention economy. Every notification, every bright red badge, is a bid for your most finite resource: your focus.

## The Dopamine Loop

Modern interfaces are designed to hook us. They use variable reward schedules—the same mechanics behind slot machines—to keep us scrolling.

### Designing Your Own Interface

What if we engineered our environment to minimize these traps? By treating our physical and digital workspace like a UI to be optimized, we can reclaim some agency.

1. **Greyscale Mode**: Turning off colors on your phone reduces the visual salience of apps.
2. **Notification Batching**: Processing "inputs" only at set times.

It's a work in progress, but the results so far are promising.
