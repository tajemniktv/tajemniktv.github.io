# The Intersection of Psychology and Tech

As a student of psychology and a digital hobbyist, I often find myself looking at software through the lens of human behavior. It's not just about code efficiency; it's about cognitive efficiency.

## Cognitive Load

Every extra click, every millisecond of delay, adds to the user's cognitive load. When we design interfaces, we are essentially managing the user's attention.

> "Good design is a lot like clear thinking made visual." - Edward Tufte
